Virtual UMASS BOSTON



This project provides a 3D Virtual tour of UMass Boston Campus Map by using the tool Unity_3D ,Oculus Rift Virtual Reality Headset and leap motion controller.The Oculus Rift Virtual Reality Headset is used to view and walk in the virtual UMB Campus and all the buildings. Users can use keyboard or other electrical input devices to walk around in the virtual campus. There are 8 buildings in the UMB campus and they have different architectures and structures, including Campus Center, Wheatley Hall, McCormack Hall, Science Center, Healey Library, Quinn Building, Clark Athletic Center, and Massachusettls Archives. Users are able to view them from outside, as well as walking inside the Science Building.
